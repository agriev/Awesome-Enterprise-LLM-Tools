## üí° Considerations for Enterprise Selection

When evaluating commercial LLM tools for enterprise use, consider:

1. **Security & Compliance**
   - SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR compliance
   - Data residency and sovereignty
   - Encryption at rest and in transit
   - Audit logging and compliance reporting

2. **Scalability & Performance**
   - SLA guarantees
   - Auto-scaling capabilities
   - Performance benchmarks
   - Multi-region support

3. **Integration**
   - SSO/SAML support
   - API capabilities
   - Enterprise directory integration
   - Existing tool ecosystem compatibility

4. **Support & SLAs**
   - Enterprise support tiers
   - Response time SLAs
   - Dedicated account management
   - Training and onboarding

5. **Cost Structure**
   - Transparent pricing
   - Enterprise pricing models
   - Usage-based vs. fixed pricing
   - Total cost of ownership

6. **Vendor Lock-in**
   - Data portability
   - API standards compliance
   - Exit strategies
   - Open standards support
